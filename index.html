Family Planning

Their Future

Many healthy couples unknowingly carry genetic variants associated with recessive conditions. Individually, these variants cause no symptoms. Together, they may affect a future child.

MyCodexa's carrier screening is designed to identify these shared risks before conception — so you can plan with your doctor from a position of knowledge, not uncertainty.
